  ## **2\. Scope of Work**

  ### **Phase 1 – Reference (Completed)**

* Holding page for the domain to keep it active and professional.  
* DNS setup and website recovery to ensure domain stability and continuity.  
* Security setup

  ### **Phase 2 – Full Website Build (Proposed)**

1. **Website Design & Development**  
   * 4–6 page responsive website (desktop, tablet, mobile).  
   * Custom layout and professional design aligned with your brand.  
   * Integration of approved content provided by Corrine.  
2. **Interactive Maps & Location**  
   * Google Maps API integration for improved map presentation.  
   * Custom styling to match website aesthetics.  
   * Embed map on Contact / Location page.  
3. **Form & Enquiry Handling**  
   * Formspree integration for secure patient enquiries.  
   * Notifications sent directly to your preferred email.  
4. **Analytics & Behaviour Tracking**  
   * Microsoft Clarity integration for website interaction insights.  
   * Heatmaps, session recordings, and interaction tracking.  
5. **SEO & Structuring**  
   * Proper page titles, meta descriptions, header hierarchy, and internal linking.  
   * Optimised URLs and alt tags for images.  
   * Basic on-page SEO for launch.  
6. **Collaborative Feedback & Revisions**  
   * Coordinated with Corrine for content and layout updates.  
   * Continuous in-document feedback is welcomed throughout development.  
   * To keep delivery on track, up to 3 major structured review rounds are included in Phase 2 scope.  
   * Additional revisions outside agreed scope are billed separately.  
7. **Responsive & Accessibility Compliance**  
   * Mobile-first design and testing across major browsers.  
   * Accessibility considerations for improved usability.
